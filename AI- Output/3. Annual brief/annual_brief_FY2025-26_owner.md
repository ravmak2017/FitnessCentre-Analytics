# Annual P&L Review — FY2025-26

## Headline
ABC Fitness Club delivered a solid year with ₹13,29,810 in total revenue and ₹7,49,283 in profit, translating to a 56.4% profit margin. July was outstanding (₹1,21,253 profit), but August derailed momentum with a loss of ₹16,495 due to a spike in miscellaneous expenses. Overall, the club remained profitable across 11 of 12 months, though year-end momentum weakened.

## The big numbers
- **Total revenue:** ₹13,29,810 (avg ₹1,10,818/month)
- **Total expenses:** ₹5,80,527 (avg ₹48,377/month)
- **Total profit:** ₹7,49,283 🟢
- **Profit margin:** 56.4%
- **Ultra membership revenue:** ₹6,67,660 (50% of total)
- **Premium membership revenue:** ₹6,62,150 (50% of total)
- **Loss months:** 1 (August 2025)

## Year highlights
- **July 2025 stellar performance:** Revenue hit ₹1,60,550 (highest of year) with profit of ₹1,21,253 and 75.5% margin — the club's best month by far.
- **Strong first quarter:** Q1 (Apr–Jun) generated ₹3,60,750 revenue and ₹2,46,166 profit at 68.2% margin, setting a confident tone.
- **Lean operating months:** January through March kept expenses controlled, with January's total expenses at just ₹28,950 (lowest of year).
- **Balanced membership mix:** Ultra and Premium revenues were nearly identical (₹6.67L each), showing stable dual-stream income.

## Year challenges
- **August 2025 loss:** Miscellaneous expenses spiked to ₹1,03,050 (7.6× normal), dragging the month into a ₹16,495 loss despite healthy ₹1,32,450 revenue.
- **Q2 margin collapse:** July's success masked a weak August and September; Q2 margin fell to 45.8% (lowest quarter).
- **Year-end weakness:** Revenue declined sharply from January onward (₹79,100 in Jan, ₹82,850 in Feb, ₹84,150 in Mar), with March profit down to ₹24,650.
- **Unexplained expense spikes:** Misc expenses spiked again in November (₹28,000) and March (₹30,000); repair costs jumped in November (₹10,160) and February (₹6,300).

## Quarter by quarter
- **Q1 (Apr–Jun):** Solid start at ₹3,60,750 revenue; profit ₹2,46,166 at strong 68.2% margin.
- **Q2 (Jul–Sep):** Peaked in July but dragged down by August loss; ₹4,13,750 revenue, ₹1,89,363 profit, 45.8% margin.
- **Q3 (Oct–Dec):** Recovered to 61.7% margin with ₹3,09,210 revenue and ₹1,90,654 profit; relatively stable.
- **Q4 (Jan–Mar):** Weakest quarter; ₹2,46,100 revenue (down 20% vs Q3), ₹1,23,100 profit, 50% margin.

## Worth checking
- **What caused the August and March expense surges?** Miscellaneous costs in August alone (₹1,03,050) wiped out profit. If these were one-time items (repairs, inventory, stock), that's manageable; if recurring, margins are at risk.
- **Why did revenue drop 35% from July to January?** July's ₹1,60,550 fell to January's ₹79,100. Was this seasonal, membership churn, or external factors? Trend into next year matters.
- **Are repair and misc expenses cyclical?** Spikes in Nov, Feb, and Mar suggest a pattern. If preventive maintenance is bunched into these months, it's worth tracking to smooth cash flow.
